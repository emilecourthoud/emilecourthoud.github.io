Short biography

I am a Consultant at SpaceTec Partners, Munich. My specialization is strategic consulting, covering market studies, trend analysis, innovation advisory, all predominantly within the space sector.

Through my education at ETH Zürich, my professional expertise and external projects I have broadened my know-how outside and around strategy, spanning from data analysis for decision making to computational mechanical modelling and biomechanical devices.
