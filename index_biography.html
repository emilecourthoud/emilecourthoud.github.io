About me

Welcome! Here is Emile.
I am a mechanical engineer by background, a management consultant by profession, a computer scientist by vocation, a dreamer by nature.
I come from Aosta Valley, a lovely spot in the heart of the Italian Alps. I grew up in the mountains, wandering in the woods, building tree houses and playing with fire. Risk and freedom, that is what I loved.
Then I discovered the city and opened my eyes. I discovered the people and opened my hearth. I discovered the world and opened my soul.
That is all, this is me.

If you want to carry on projects and collaborations together, contact me via Linkedin.
If you want to know me, drop a message on Telegram / WhatsApp (+490782495566).
If you want to meet me, just let me know.
